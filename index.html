About Oregon Studio Group

Over 25 Years of Capturing Pure Photographic Masterpieces

Led by premier Medford photographer John Neilson, Oregon Studio Group LLC blends decades of technical expertise with elite creative visions. Our close-knit crew—featuring master lighting technicians, makeup artists, and art directors—is completely dedicated to excellence, ensuring every single milestone radiates with authentic life and prestige.

We prioritize deep communication with our clients, treating each campaign and personal session as custom fine-art documentation. Step in front of our lenses to experience the most empowering and beautifully structured photoshoot experience in Southern Oregon.
